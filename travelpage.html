New York CityJanuary 2014

We decided to make a trip to NYC on MLK weekend before we moved too far away for it to be a simple trip. Ellie planned a great trip, we did all the touristy stuff. I fed a squirrel in Central Park, we saw Phantom of the Opera, and the statue of the liberty. The view out our hotel room window was pretty great. All around the trip was as successful as a weekend trip could be.

SeattleJuly 2013

Ellie had an internship with Boeing for the summer and I was still in Columbus but I made the flight out for the 4th of July weekend. Beautiful weather, a few happy gatherings with friends from home and some ubiquitous tourist stops like the Pike Place Market and the Space Needle made the trip a memorable one. It can't be overstated how much this trip contributed to our decision to move to Seattle after graduation.
